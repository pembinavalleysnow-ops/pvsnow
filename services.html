What is a Season Pass?

A Season Pass is your guarantee of a clear driveway effectively all winter long. Unlike per-push services that charge you based on the storm's severity, our flat-rate pass covers you for the entire season (Nov 1 - Apr 15).

We dispatch our fleet automatically whenever snow accumulation reaches your plan's trigger amount (5cm, 3cm, etc.). This means you never have to call us. You wake up, and it's done. We also clean up the ridge left by city plows, ensuring you can always get out.
